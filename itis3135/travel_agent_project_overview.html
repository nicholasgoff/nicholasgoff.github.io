Travel Agent Project

Overview

My mom just recently became a travel agent. This site will try to help her reach more people who are looking for a travel agent to find the best deals for them. It is catered more towards cruises but she can help with any travel needs.

Categories

  • Home Page: an introduction to your travel agent and why you should book with her plus contact information.
  • Royal Caribbean Page: Provides photos and insights on what you can do with royal plus deals they offer.
  • Carnival Page: Provides photos and insights on what you can do with carnival plus deals they offer.
  • MSC Page: Provides photos and insights on what you can do with MSC plus deals they offer.
  • Other Cruises Page: Provides photo and information on a variety of other cruises and what they have to offer.

Interactivity

  • A quick link form that you provide your name and what you are looking for that will send an email to the agent.
  • A slideshow of photos on each page ranging from their ships to ports and activities.
